How it Works

256 colors are generated each week. The Leaderboard page sorts the results by the average score of each color.

Author

rateMyColor was written by Aram Peres. The core functionality was completed in about 4 hours, and additional UI improvements were brought subsequently.

Matei Cheong gave the original idea for a website about voting for colors.
